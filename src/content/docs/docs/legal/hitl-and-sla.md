# HITL and SLA

While AgentBureau aims for maximum automation, certain services require **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)** intervention to ensure legal compliance and quality.

### Turnaround Times (SLAs)

| Action | Targeted Response | Maximum Delay |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| **Fax Transmission** | < 5 mins | 2 hours |
| **Letter Printing** | Same business day | 24 hours |
| **GmbH Document Review** | 4 hours | 1 business day |
| **Support Inquiries** | 2 hours | 1 business day |

### Manual Review (HITL)

Our human operators perform the following checks:
- **Compliance**: Ensuring content does not violate German law (e.g., hate speech, fraud).
- **Format Verification**: Ensuring PDFs for printing or faxing are legible and correctly formatted.
- **Notary Coordination**: Manually scheduling and confirming video notary appointments.

### Refund Triggers

We value transparency. Refunds of USDC are triggered automatically or manually under the following conditions:
1. **Service Failure**: If a fax or letter cannot be sent after all retry attempts.
2. **SLA Breach**: If a manual review takes longer than the maximum delay specified above.
3. **Invalid KYC**: If a KYC process is rejected by the provider (share capital escrow is refunded).

*Note: Gas fees paid for the initial transaction are non-refundable as they are paid to the network, not AgentBureau.*
